Ministry Impact
We are deeply committed to responsible stewardship; over 96% of all resources entrusted to us are directly channeled into ministry programs, ensuring maximum impact in meeting global physical and spiritual needs.

Volunteer Hours
Thanks to the compassion and dedication of our volunteers, we've accumulated over 7,000 hours of impactful service, transforming lives through direct involvement in health care missions and community outreach.

Countries Served
Our outreach programs have expanded globally, delivering medical care, vital supplies, and the message of hope to underserved communities across more than 30 countries around the world.

Who We Are

Caring Partners International (CPI) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit Christian ministry that equips churches to meet healthcare needs and share the Gospel message of salvation. Learn more about our mission and how we serve communities worldwide.

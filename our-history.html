Our History

Since our founding in 1992, Caring Partners International has grown from a small ministry with a big vision into a global mission — faithfully serving communities in need through Evangelism Through Medicine®.

1992

The Beginning of the Ministry

Caring Partners International (CPI) was established in 1992 in Ohio as a 501(c)(3) Christian ministry. Its primary mission is to enable and equip local churches to meet the physical and spiritual needs of the lost and suffering around the world. Since its founding, the ministry has focused solely on “Evangelism Through Medicine®” programs carried out worldwide in and through the local church.

1992 – 2008

Growing Through Challenges

For more than 15 years, Caring Partners operated in rented warehouse space in a very old building in downtown Middletown, Ohio. The building was an abandoned paper mill, and the conditions were less than optimal for storing and processing medical supplies and equipment. Despite space, environment, and infrastructure challenges, God blessed the ministry, which continued growing and expanding. Medications, medical supplies, and equipment were delivered to church mission projects worldwide, including Tanzania, Belarus, Mexico, Cuba, India, Kenya, and several sites in the USA, including relief efforts for Hurricane Katrina.

2008

A God-Given Facility

The ministry was incredibly blessed when a generous local donor heard about God’s work through CPI and its partner churches and felt burdened to help. The donor granted the ministry enough funds to purchase their current 10,000-square-foot facility to carry on the ministry’s Kingdom impact. Caring Partners has continued to grow and expand its “Evangelism Through Medicine®” ministries and programs through this debt-free facility.

2011

Launching the Tri-State Care Network

Caring Partners launched the Tri-State Care Network in southwest Ohio in 2011. The Network is a coalition of churches, ministries, and faith-based community organizations that serve the ‘least of these’ in the most needed areas in our local communities.

Present

Continuing the Mission Worldwide

Through the generosity and support of donors, volunteers, and churches, Caring Partners International continues to grow and expand its programs, delivering medical aid and the hope of the Gospel to communities in need worldwide. The commitment to “Evangelism Through Medicine®” remains at the heart of everything we do.
